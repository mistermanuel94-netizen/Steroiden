## What is Follistatin 344?

**Follistatin 344** is a naturally occurring glycoprotein that functions as a potent antagonist of myostatin (GDF-8) and activins within the TGF-β superfamily. By binding and neutralising these growth-inhibiting factors, Follistatin removes natural constraints on muscle growth, making it one of the most important research tools in muscle biology. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Follistatin](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=follistatin+344){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

The 344-amino acid isoform is the full-length variant with the broadest tissue distribution and biological activity spectrum.
