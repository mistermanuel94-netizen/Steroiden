## What is Crystagen?

**Crystagen** is a synthetic bioregulatory tripeptide with the sequence Thr-Glu-Lys (TEK), developed as part of Professor Vladimir Khavinson's bioregulatory peptide programme at the St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology. Crystagen specifically targets immune cells, modulating gene expression in lymphocytes and natural killer cells to promote balanced and effective immune responses.
