## What is Thymagen?

**Thymagen** is a synthetic bioregulatory dipeptide composed of L-glutamic acid and L-tryptophan (Glu-Trp). Developed as part of Professor Vladimir Khavinson's bioregulatory peptide research at the St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology, Thymagen specifically targets thymic tissue to modulate immune function. It promotes the restoration of thymic activity and supports T-lymphocyte maturation and differentiation. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Thymagen](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=thymagen+bioregulator+peptide){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
