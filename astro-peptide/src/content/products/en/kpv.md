## What is KPV?

**KPV** (Lys-Pro-Val) is a naturally occurring tripeptide derived from the C-terminal end of alpha-melanocyte stimulating hormone (α-MSH). Despite its small size, KPV possesses potent anti-inflammatory properties, inhibiting NF-κB signalling and reducing pro-inflammatory cytokine production without activating melanocortin receptors. For published research, see [PubMed studies on KPV](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=KPV+peptide+anti-inflammatory){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

KPV's ability to suppress inflammation without hormonal side effects makes it particularly valuable for gastrointestinal and mucosal immunity research.
