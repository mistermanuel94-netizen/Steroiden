## What is DNSP-11?

**DNSP-11** (Dopamine Neuron Stimulating Peptide-11) is an 11-amino acid synthetic peptide derived from the pro-region of glial cell line-derived neurotrophic factor (proGDNF). Unlike mature GDNF, DNSP-11 promotes dopaminergic neuron survival through a mechanism independent of the canonical GFRα1/RET signalling complex. This makes it a uniquely valuable research tool for studying neuroprotection in Parkinson's disease models. For published research, see [PubMed studies on DNSP-11](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=DNSP-11+dopamine+neuron){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

DNSP-11 has demonstrated significant neurotrophic activity in both in vitro dopaminergic cell cultures and in vivo rodent models of nigrostriatal degeneration.
