## What is Prostamax?

**Prostamax** is a synthetic bioregulatory tetrapeptide with the sequence Lys-Glu-Asp-Pro (KEDP), developed as part of Professor Vladimir Khavinson's bioregulatory peptide programme at the St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology. Prostamax specifically targets prostate tissue, modulating gene expression in prostatic epithelial cells to promote normal cell proliferation, androgen receptor homeostasis, and protection against age-related and inflammatory prostatic changes. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Prostamax](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=prostamax+bioregulator+peptide+prostate){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
