## What is Hexarelin?

**Hexarelin** (Examorelin) is a synthetic hexapeptide growth hormone secretagogue and one of the most potent GHRPs available for research. Beyond its powerful GH-releasing activity, Hexarelin exhibits unique cardioprotective properties mediated through CD36 scavenger receptor binding, making it a versatile research tool spanning endocrinology and cardiovascular science. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Hexarelin](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=hexarelin){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

Hexarelin's dual mechanism — pituitary GH release via GHS-R1a and cardiac protection via CD36 — distinguishes it from other growth hormone secretagogues.
