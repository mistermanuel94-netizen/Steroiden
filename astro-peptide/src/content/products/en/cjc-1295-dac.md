## What is CJC-1295 with DAC?

**CJC-1295 with DAC** (Drug Affinity Complex) is a synthetic 30-amino acid peptide analogue of growth hormone releasing hormone (GHRH) modified with a Drug Affinity Complex that enables binding to circulating albumin. This modification extends the peptide's half-life from approximately 30 minutes to around 8 days, making it one of the longest-acting GHRH analogues available for research. For published research, see [PubMed studies on CJC-1295](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=CJC-1295){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
