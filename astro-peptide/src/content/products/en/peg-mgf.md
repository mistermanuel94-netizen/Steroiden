## What is PEG-MGF?

**PEG-MGF** (PEGylated Mechano Growth Factor) is a chemically modified form of MGF conjugated with a polyethylene glycol (PEG) moiety to extend its biological half-life. While native MGF has an extremely short half-life of only minutes in biological systems, PEGylation dramatically improves stability by shielding the peptide from enzymatic degradation. For published research, see [PubMed studies on PEG-MGF](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=PEG-MGF+mechano+growth+factor){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

PEG-MGF retains the satellite cell activation properties of native MGF while offering a sustained-release pharmacokinetic profile, making it a valuable research tool for studying prolonged growth factor signalling.
