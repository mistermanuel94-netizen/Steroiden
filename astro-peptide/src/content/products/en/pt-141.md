## What is PT-141?

**PT-141** (Bremelanotide) is a synthetic cyclic heptapeptide melanocortin receptor agonist originally derived from Melanotan-2. Unlike its parent compound, PT-141 was specifically developed to target melanocortin-3 and melanocortin-4 receptors (MC3R/MC4R) with selectivity, making it a valuable research tool for studying central nervous system pathways involved in sexual function. For published research, see [PubMed studies on PT-141](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=PT-141+bremelanotide){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

PT-141 operates through a mechanism distinct from PDE5 inhibitors, acting centrally through melanocortin receptor activation rather than peripheral vasodilation.
