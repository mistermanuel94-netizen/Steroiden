## What is Tesamorelin?

**Tesamorelin** (TH9507) is a synthetic 44-amino acid analogue of human growth hormone releasing hormone (GHRH) with a trans-3-hexenoic acid modification at the N-terminus. This modification enhances enzymatic resistance and increases potency compared to native GHRH. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Tesamorelin](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=tesamorelin){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

Tesamorelin is unique among GHRH analogues for its extensive clinical data on visceral adipose tissue reduction, making it particularly valuable for metabolic and lipodystrophy research.
