## What is ARA-290?

**ARA-290** (Cibinetide) is an 11-amino acid synthetic peptide derived from the three-dimensional structure of erythropoietin (EPO). It was rationally designed to selectively activate the innate repair receptor (IRR)—a heteromer of the erythropoietin receptor (EPOR) and β-common receptor (CD131)—without stimulating classical erythropoiesis. For published research, see [PubMed studies on ARA-290 Cibinetide](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=ARA-290+cibinetide){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

This selectivity makes ARA-290 a valuable research tool for studying tissue-protective, anti-inflammatory, and reparative mechanisms that are independent of red blood cell production.
