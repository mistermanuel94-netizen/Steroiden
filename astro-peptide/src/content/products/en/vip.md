## What is VIP?

**VIP** (Vasoactive Intestinal Peptide) is a 28-amino acid neuropeptide belonging to the glucagon/secretin superfamily. Originally isolated from porcine small intestine, VIP is widely distributed in the central and peripheral nervous systems, where it functions as a neurotransmitter, neuromodulator, and potent vasodilator. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Vasoactive Intestinal Peptide](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=vasoactive+intestinal+peptide+VIP){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

VIP plays critical roles in circadian rhythm synchronisation, gastrointestinal motility, immunomodulation, and neuroprotection, making it one of the most extensively studied neuropeptides in biomedical research.
