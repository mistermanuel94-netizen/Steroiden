## What is Testagen?

**Testagen** is a synthetic bioregulatory tetrapeptide developed as part of Professor Vladimir Khavinson's bioregulatory peptide programme at the St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology. Testagen specifically targets testicular tissue, modulating gene expression in Leydig and Sertoli cells to support reproductive endocrine function, steroidogenesis, and spermatogenesis. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Testagen](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=testagen+bioregulator+peptide){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

Testagen represents an important research tool for studying testicular bioregulation, male reproductive ageing, and the effects of short peptides on gonadal function.
