## What is Colivelin?

**Colivelin** is a potent synthetic hybrid neuroprotective peptide engineered by fusing the core active sequence of activity-dependent neurotrophic factor (ADNF-9) with a derivative of humanin (AGA-HNG17), creating a dual-mechanism neuroprotectant with broader efficacy than either parent peptide. It was developed by Matsuoka and colleagues as a research tool for studying neuronal survival against multiple neurotoxic insults associated with Alzheimer's disease. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Colivelin](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=colivelin+neuroprotective){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

Colivelin has demonstrated femtomolar-range neuroprotective activity in vitro, making it one of the most potent neuroprotective peptides described in the literature.
