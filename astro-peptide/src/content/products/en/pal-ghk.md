## What is PAL-GHK?

**PAL-GHK** (Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1) is a lipopeptide composed of palmitic acid covalently conjugated to the bioactive tripeptide glycyl-histidyl-lysine (GHK). The palmitoyl modification enhances lipophilicity and dermal bioavailability, making PAL-GHK a widely used research tool for studying collagen biosynthesis, extracellular matrix remodelling, and skin ageing biology. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=palmitoyl+tripeptide-1+GHK){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

PAL-GHK is one of the most extensively studied cosmetic peptides, with demonstrated activity in stimulating fibroblast collagen production and modulating TGF-β signalling pathways.
