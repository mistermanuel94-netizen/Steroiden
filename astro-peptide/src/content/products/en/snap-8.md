## What is SNAP-8?

**SNAP-8** (Acetyl Octapeptide-3, also known as Acetyl Glutamyl Heptapeptide-1) is a biomimetic octapeptide that modulates SNARE complex assembly at the neuromuscular junction. Developed as an extension of the hexapeptide Argireline, SNAP-8 contains two additional amino acids that enhance its competitive inhibition of SNAP-25 within the SNARE complex, making it one of the most studied topical anti-wrinkle peptides. For published research, see [PubMed studies on SNAP-8](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=SNAP-8+acetyl+octapeptide+SNARE){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

SNAP-8 represents a non-invasive, peptide-based approach to modulating expression lines, offering a research alternative to botulinum toxin-based mechanisms.
