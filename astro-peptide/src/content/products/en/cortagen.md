## What is Cortagen?

**Cortagen** is a synthetic bioregulatory tetrapeptide developed as part of Professor Vladimir Khavinson's bioregulatory peptide programme at the St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology. Cortagen specifically targets cerebral cortex tissue, modulating gene expression in cortical neurons to promote neuroprotection, synaptic plasticity, and cognitive function pathways. For published research, see [PubMed studies on Cortagen](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=cortagen+bioregulator+peptide+brain){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
